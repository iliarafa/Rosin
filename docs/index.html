Rosin Ai is a multi-LLM verification tool that combats AI hallucinations by running queries through multiple language models in sequence. Each stage verifies and refines the previous output to distill truth from AI responses.

> [PRIVACY POLICY]

01 Overview

Rosin Ai ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

02 Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • No account or sign-up is required
  • No analytics or tracking frameworks are used
  • No cookies or device identifiers are collected
  • No usage data is sent to our servers
03 API Keys

The App requires API keys to connect to third-party language model providers (Anthropic, Google Gemini, xAI). These keys are stored locally on your device using the iOS Keychain with the kSecAttrAccessibleWhenUnlockedThisDeviceOnly protection level. API keys are never transmitted to any server other than the respective LLM provider's API endpoint.

04 Third-Party Services

When you submit a query, the App sends your input directly from your device to the LLM providers you have configured. Each provider's own privacy policy governs how they handle your data:

  • Anthropic — anthropic.com/privacy
  • Google Gemini — ai.google.dev/terms
  • xAI — x.ai/legal/privacy-policy
05 Data Retention

The App does not persist your queries or verification results. All data exists only in memory during your active session and is discarded when the App is closed.

06 Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

07 Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

08 Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at iliasr@me.com.
